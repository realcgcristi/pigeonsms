# code of conduct

pigeonsms is a small, friendly, privacy-first project. the bar is simple: **don't be a jerk.**

## the vibe

- be kind and patient — this is a hobby project maintained by one person in their spare time
- assume good faith; critique code and ideas, not people
- newcomers and dumb questions are welcome. everyone started somewhere
- keep it on-topic and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions

## not okay

harassment, hate speech, slurs, personal attacks, doxxing, sexual content or advances, deliberate trolling, or generally making the space hostile. also: don't spam, don't demand free labor, don't be entitled about review timelines.

## scope

this applies everywhere the project lives — issues, pull requests, discussions, commit messages, and any official channel.

## enforcement

if someone's breaking this, or you feel unsafe, email **realcgcristi@getswift.cloud**. reports are handled privately. i can and will warn, edit/remove content, or ban people from the project — my call, and i'll try to be fair about it.

---

this is loosely based on the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), kept short because it's a small project. the gist: treat people well.
